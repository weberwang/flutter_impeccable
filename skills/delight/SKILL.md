# Add Delight To Flutter UI

## Mandatory Preparation

Invoke `/impeccable` first and decide whether delight is appropriate for the target surface.

## Purpose

Introduce moments of warmth, completion, and satisfaction in Flutter UI without distracting from task completion.

## Good Delight Moments In This Repo

- empty states that encourage the next step
- success confirmations after save, submit, or approval actions
- subtle button or toggle feedback
- tasteful onboarding or first-run guidance
- reassuring transitions during stressful flows

## Flutter Delight Toolkit

- `AnimatedSwitcher`
- subtle `Hero` continuity
- lightweight haptics where appropriate
- confirmation visuals tied to success state
- gentle progress feedback
- polished icons or illustrations on empty states

## Rules

- keep delight short and secondary to the task
- make success feel reassuring, not childish
- use copy and motion that fit a professional logistics product
- prefer one meaningful moment over many gimmicks

## Avoid

- jokey copy in high-stakes flows
- confetti or celebratory effects on routine operational steps
- decorative animations that slow form submission or list scanning
- delight that appears more important than the actual result

## Output

When applying this skill, specify:

- the exact user moment being enhanced
- what emotional friction or deadness it fixes
- the Flutter widget or interaction pattern used to implement it
