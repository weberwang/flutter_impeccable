# Make Flutter UI Bolder

## Mandatory Preparation

Invoke `/impeccable` first and confirm whether the target is an overview, onboarding, dashboard, or other surface where stronger visual emphasis is appropriate.

## Purpose

Add confidence and visual impact to Flutter UI that currently feels bland, timid, or equal-weight everywhere.

## What Bolder Means In This Repo

- stronger hierarchy
- clearer focal point
- more decisive section emphasis
- tighter typography contrast
- more memorable but still credible enterprise presentation

It does not mean turning a transactional page into a marketing poster.

## Flutter Levers

- stronger title and metric hierarchy using existing text styles or controlled extensions
- bolder section framing through spacing, surface contrast, or accent treatment
- clearer CTA priority
- more distinctive icon or illustration treatment on low-risk surfaces
- carefully chosen motion or transition emphasis where it improves orientation

## Best Targets

- dashboard hero sections
- onboarding or first-run surfaces
- summary or overview cards with clear priority
- success surfaces where reinforcement helps confidence

## Risky Targets

- dense approval forms
- compliance-heavy details
- pages with many destructive or high-stakes decisions

## Output

For each recommendation, specify:

- what is too weak today
- what gets amplified
- how the hierarchy changes
- how to keep the page consistent with the rest of the app

## Never

- use louder color as a substitute for structure
- make every section bold
- import generic startup-dashboard aesthetics into the logistics product
