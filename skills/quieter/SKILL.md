# Quiet Down Flutter UI

## Mandatory Preparation

Invoke `/impeccable` first and confirm which elements currently feel too loud: color, spacing, cards, typography, or motion.

## Purpose

Reduce visual intensity while preserving clarity and confidence.

## Common Flutter Problems This Skill Targets

- too many tinted cards and badges competing together
- oversized headers or metrics with no breathing room
- repeated shadows, borders, and containers
- motion or transitions that make routine tasks feel slower
- saturated colors applied to non-primary elements

## Quieting Moves

- reduce the number of surfaces competing for attention
- let neutral backgrounds do more work
- simplify card structure and border treatment
- tighten title scale if it dominates too much
- reduce motion distance, duration, or frequency
- make one priority area strong and let everything else recede

## Good Outcome In This Repo

- the screen feels calmer and easier to scan
- important statuses still stand out
- the page looks more trustworthy and less theatrical
- users spend less effort separating signal from decoration

## Never

- flatten the hierarchy until nothing stands out
- make a high-information page look empty or timid
- remove meaningful state cues just to reduce intensity
